Just a Little Bit About Us

What We Do

TravelWiz is a full-service travel agency and web magazine offering travel agency, news, feature articles and reviews on all aspects of the travel experience.

Agency and Travel Services

TravelWiz booking covers air, cruise and train travel, rental vehicles, and hotel accomodations all over the world through its advanced award-winning platform. Access innovative search tools to help you discover the best packages for your budget and dream vacation. We can arrange all the logistics of distance, time, and scheduling appropriate for your favorite destinations and attractions.

News and Feaure Articles

Our feature articles and reviews examine the topics that matter to travelers. Where to sleep, where to eat, what to see, best times to visit, and when to head home. Special investigative reports illuminate the worldly events affecting travel, from changes in energy prices to improvements in technology, as well as the exigencies of pandemics and world conflict. At TravelWiz you can persuse these weekly articles for all your travel information and enjoyment:

Travel Life
Articles exploring travel news and beyond, including the highlights of cities, history and monuments, museums, parks andrecreation, food and cultural life at your favorite destinations.
Entertainment
Features on artists, performers, and events happening at cities across the Americas, Asia, Australia, and Europe.
Technology
Reviews of new technology and accessories that can ease your travel life and enhance your vacation experience.
Industry
Reports of changes happening in the travel industry, including announcements, health and safety, mergers and acquistions, and more.
